RIEL

RIEL

STORY

Joining from Russia, Riel is a young agent with sharp intelligence and quick reflexes. At the age of 20, he has mastered the art of advanced technology and close combat. With the ability to control the battlefield through electronic devices, he can disrupt enemies without being detected, making his an extremely dangerous ally. As a quick-thinking and agile commander, Riel is always one step ahead, ready to change the course of battle with precise strategies and exceptional skills.

RIEL is a Duelist Agent with self-sufficient fragging capabilities, using his abilities to outmaneuver enemies and secure frags. He can dash to avoid danger or quickly close the gap on an opponent to take them down. His ultimate ability, Empress, significantly enhances her combat abilities, making her a threat to even the most skilled enemy players.

SUYO

SUYO

STORY

Born and raised in the heart of Germany, Suyo honed his skills as a master tactician through years of disciplined study and real-world experience. His ability to analyze, strategize, and adapt has earned him a reputation as an unrivaled tactical mastermind. Whether in the boardroom or the battlefield, Suyo's plans are precise, effective, and often unpredictable. His focus on efficiency and results is tempered by his ability to inspire and lead, making him a valuable asset in any team.

Suyo is a Controller Agent who can manipulate the battlefield to his advantage. He can use his abilities to block sightlines, cut off enemy movement, and control the flow of battle. His ultimate ability, Viper's Pit, creates a large toxic cloud that obscures vision and damages enemies who linger within it. Suyo is a master of deception and can use his abilities to outsmart and outmaneuver his opponents.

JPN, TOKYO

KEANT

KEANT

STORY

Hailing from the neon-lit streets of Japan, Keant has perfected the art of theft through years of rigorous practice and cunning. Born into a world of shadows, he quickly adapted to the ways of stealth and precision, earning his reputation as an unparalleled thief. No vault is secure, no guard is safe, and no target is out of reach when Keant is in play. He's a calculated risk-taker with an eye for detail, always staying one step ahead of the competition.

Keant is a Sentinel Agent who can lock down areas of the map and provide valuable information to his team. He can use his abilities to slow down enemy pushes, reveal enemy locations, and protect his teammates from harm. His ultimate ability, Lockdown, creates a large field that detains enemies caught within it, making them vulnerable to attack. Keant is a reliable and supportive agent who can help his team secure victory through careful planning and strategic play.

CESTER

CESTER

STORY

Born in the vibrant streets of Sao Paulo, Cester grew up witnessing both the vitality and struggles of his community. Determined to make a difference, he trained as a medic, mastering both conventional and battlefield medicine. From urban clinics to war zones, Cester has been a beacon of hope, patching up the wounded and saving lives under the most extreme conditions. His calm demeanor under pressure and unshakable resolve make him an invaluable ally in any crisis.

Cester is an initiator agent who can disrupt enemy positions and create opportunities for his team. He can use his abilities to clear out enemy defenses, gather information on enemy positions, and create space for his team to push forward. His ultimate ability, Showstopper, launches a powerful explosive that can clear out multiple enemies at once. Cester is a versatile agent who can adapt to any situation and help his team secure victory through careful planning and strategic play.

JIMO

STORY

Hailing from the bustling streets of Shanghai, Jimo's calm demeanor and sharp intellect have always set him apart. Trained in both crisis negotiation and explosive ordnance disposal (EOD), Jimo combines psychological acumen with technical expertise to defuse both literal and figurative ticking time bombs. Whether talking down a volatile adversary or dismantling a high-tech explosive, Jimo's precision and focus make him an unparalleled asset in high-stakes situations.

Jimo is a half sentinel and controller agent who can provide valuable information to his team and disrupt enemy positions. He can use his abilities to gather information on enemy positions, slow down enemy pushes, and control the flow of battle. His ultimate ability, Lockdown, creates a large field that detains enemies caught within it, making them vulnerable to attack. Jimo is a reliable and supportive agent who can help his team secure victory through careful planning and strategic play.
